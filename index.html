"Food at your Steps"

Rural Entrepreneurship Division (ProjectRED) is CEL's rural entrepreneurship project, running in Rajasthan and Goa, and looks to turn our rural location into an advantage, by looking to assist and setup rural enterprises and in the process give a sustainable source of income to village folk in surrounding areas. RED is working towards creation of social businesses amongst the stake holders of the society who act as change makers in the long run.

Project Udaan is focused around enhancing the lives of BITsians and those around us.Project Udaan focuses around making food chains in and around BITs more accessible to the students and faculty. We, here at Project Udaan, have started a delivery system, one which delivers food right at the doorsteps of the customers. In doing so, we also hope to employ local residents in the town of Pilani and help employment rates grow. We have started small, delivering from only in-campus food stalls but hope to expand to encompass all chains in Pilani. Due to Project Udaan, there will be no need to brave the extreme climates and enjoy the pleasures of fine dining right in the comforts of your room. We, here at Project Udaan, strive for excellence and hope to provide a customer-friendly service with 100% reliability. We only promise what we can deliver and nothing more. We have invested in a better and more comfortable future and strive to keep doing so.
